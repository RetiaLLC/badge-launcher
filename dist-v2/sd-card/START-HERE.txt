BADGE LAUNCHER v2 "TOUCH" — START HERE
=======================================

Your badge is a shapeshifter. This card holds everything it can become.

HOW TO SWITCH FIRMWARE (no computer needed)
  1. Hold UP on the d-pad.
  2. Tap RESET (side button), KEEP HOLDING UP about 1 second.
  3. Menu appears. UP/DOWN to choose, A to install & boot, B to rescan.
  Switching takes ~10 seconds. You cannot brick the badge doing this —
  if anything goes wrong, hold-UP always brings the menu back.

WHAT'S ON THE MENU
  meshcore-messenger   Full-color TOUCH mesh messenger (MeshCore). This is
                       what the badge boots out of the box. Tap through the
                       wizard, name yourself, message other badges over LoRa.
  meshtastic-mui       Full-color TOUCH Meshtastic. The other big mesh
                       ecosystem — set your REGION (US) on first boot, then
                       find your friends on LongFast.
  meshtastic-standard  Classic button-driven Meshtastic (pairs with the
                       phone app over Bluetooth).
  meshtastic-lowpower  Same, tuned for battery life (BLE/LEDs off by
                       default, black idle screen).
  anemoia-nes          NES emulator. Games live in /roms on this card.
                       First launch of a game copies it into flash
                       (a few seconds) — after that it's instant.
                       Tap BOOT=START, hold BOOT=pause menu, BOOT+B=SELECT.
  wled-pride           The ear NeoPixels, straight into a Pride rainbow.

RULES OF THE RADIO
  * NEVER transmit without an antenna attached — it can damage the radio.
  * Set your region before meshing (MUI asks on first boot; the messenger
    ships tuned for US 902-928 MHz).
  * Your settings survive switching: each firmware keeps its own private
    slice of flash. Swap all day; your identity, keys, and chats stay put.

WANT DOOM?
  Doom needs the "Classic" launcher image (its game data needs the flash
  space this card's touch apps use). Grab launcher v1 from the
  DefconBadge2026 releases page.

LEARN THE MESH
  Open MESH-101.md on this card. Two badges + ten minutes = your first
  mesh network, then the same radio speaking a second protocol.

Add your own firmware: drop any app-format .bin built for the launcher's
partition table into /firmware — it shows up in the menu by filename.
